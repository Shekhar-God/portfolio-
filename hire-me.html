Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. A simple landing page might take 3-4 days to 1 week, while a full website can take 1-2 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline after reviewing your project requirements.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! I include up to 3 rounds of revisions with every project to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. Additional revisions can be discussed if needed.

Can you help with hosting and domain setup?

Absolutely! I can guide you through the process of selecting a hosting provider and setting up your domain. I'll also help you deploy your website and ensure everything works perfectly.

What makes you different from other developers?

As a young developer, I bring fresh perspectives and stay current with the latest trends. I'm passionate about every project and offer competitive rates while maintaining high quality standards.

How do we communicate during the project?

I believe in clear communication throughout the project. We can use email, video calls, or your preferred communication method. I'll provide regular updates and seek your feedback at key milestones.
